Frequently Asked Questions

What is ZeaCRM and what does it offer? +

ZeaCRM is an all-in-one business management platform that combines CRM, marketing automation, appointment scheduling, and reporting tools in a single dashboard. It's designed to help businesses manage customer relationships, automate marketing, and streamline operations.

How do I import my existing contacts into ZeaCRM? +

You can import contacts via CSV file directly from the Contacts section. ZeaCRM also offers direct integrations with popular platforms and website forms for automatic lead import. Check our "Getting Started" guide for step-by-step instructions.

Can I set up automated SMS campaigns for customer follow-up? +

Yes! ZeaCRM includes powerful SMS automation. You can create drip campaigns that trigger based on customer behavior, send appointment reminders, and enable two-way texting. All SMS conversations are automatically logged in the contact's profile.

What kind of reporting and analytics does ZeaCRM provide? +

ZeaCRM offers comprehensive reporting including lead source tracking, conversion rates, pipeline performance, team productivity metrics, and marketing campaign ROI. Custom dashboards can be created to monitor your most important business KPIs.

How do I connect my website to capture leads automatically? +

ZeaCRM provides embeddable forms and a website builder. You can add forms to any website using a simple script, or use our built-in website templates. Leads captured through these forms automatically flow into your CRM and trigger assigned workflows.

Is there training available for new users? +

Yes! We offer multiple training options: 1) Video tutorial library, 2) Weekly live training sessions, 3) Onboarding assistance for new customers, and 4) Advanced training for teams. Check the "Video Training Library" section above to get started.
